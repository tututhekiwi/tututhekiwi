About

Tutu the Kiwi is a pāua-hued adventurer with a bright orange ngutu and feet, a woven piupiu, and the cheekiest tongue in Aotearoa. We craft stories, images, and merch that celebrate whānau, reo, and manaakitanga.

Design rule: Tutu’s base model is locked. We add new scenes, props, and adventures — never altering his body, proportions, or colours.

Values

  • Ako & aroha — learning with love
  • Whānau first — community, care, connection
  • Pono — truth, craft, and quality
