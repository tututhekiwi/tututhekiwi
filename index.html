About Tutu

Tutu is a kiwi with pāua-inspired blue-green feathers, bright orange beak and feet, a woven piupiu, and eyes wide with wonder. This world blends Māori artistry with playful storytelling — all guided by strict design rules so Tutu always looks exactly right.

The kaupapa: creativity, mātauranga, and kindness — inviting tamariki and big kids alike to explore, learn, and give back.
