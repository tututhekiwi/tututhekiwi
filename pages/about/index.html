About Tutu the Kiwi

Tutu the Kiwi is a celebration of Aotearoa — through storytelling, illustration, and creativity. Each project, from books to art, is made with aroha for tamariki and whānau around the world.

Our Mission

To inspire curiosity, creativity, and connection through stories that reflect our whenua, te taiao (the natural world), and Māori values. Tutu reminds us that kindness, learning, and laughter can be found wherever we explore.

Created by Hamaka Murray

Hamaka is a storyteller and designer from Aotearoa who founded Tutu the Kiwi as part of a broader creative kaupapa to celebrate Māori identity and storytelling in digital spaces. The journey continues to grow with every book, every illustration, and every shared smile.
