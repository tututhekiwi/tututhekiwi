Pātai auau (FAQ)

What do I receive when I adopt a feather?
A personalised digital certificate, a thank-you on our Feather Wall (unless you prefer to remain anonymous), and extras by tier (like exclusive wallpapers). Whole Wing supporters are acknowledged on a supporters page.
Where does my support go?
Into creating and sharing stories, art, and learning resources for tamariki—illustrations, web features, and community kōrero grounded in Aotearoa.
Can I gift an adoption?
Āe! Mention it in your email and we’ll put the certificate in your recipient’s name.
When will payments go live?
Soon. We’re wiring a secure checkout. Everyone who registers interest here will be notified first.
