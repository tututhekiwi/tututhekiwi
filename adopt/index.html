Adopt a Feather

Adopt a virtual feather to show your support. Your name (or whānau name) will appear on our acknowledgements wall.

  1. Click Donate on Givealittle to koha (choose any amount).
  2. In the message, include your preferred name for the wall.
  3. We’ll update the wall weekly as feathers are adopted.
