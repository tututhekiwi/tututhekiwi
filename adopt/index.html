About Tutu the Kiwi

Beneath the moonlit canopy of Aotearoa, Tutu the Kiwi glows with colours of the pāua shell — greens, blues, and flickers of gold. He’s curious, cheeky, and always searching for his next story.

Tutu’s world is woven from te ao Māori — full of connection, respect, and wonder. Each feather tells a story of collaboration, learning, and creativity. Through art, design, and storytelling, this kaupapa shares what it means to explore with courage and kindness.

Every illustration and word is created with aroha, guided by tikanga and authenticity. The goal is simple: to inspire tamariki and grown-ups alike to create, question, and see the world through a brighter lens.

Tutu’s journey began as an idea — a spark between imagination and culture — and now grows into books, art, merchandise, and community collaborations that honour Aotearoa’s spirit.
